How to Care for Your Coody Inflatable Tent

Taking care of your Coody inflatable tent is essential to ensure it lasts many years. Fortunately, these tents are designed to be low-maintenance, but you can still do a few things to keep them in top condition.

After each use, make sure to clean your tent thoroughly. Use a mild soap and water solution to remove dirt and debris from the fabric. Avoid using harsh chemicals, as they can damage the waterproof coating. Once cleaned, allow the tent to dry completely before packing it away to prevent mold and mildew from developing.

When storing your Coody inflatable tent, ensure it's packed in a cool, dry place. Avoid exposing the tent to direct sunlight for extended periods, as UV rays can weaken the fabric over time. If you're storing the tent for an extended period, consider partially inflating it to prevent the air beams from sticking together.

Regularly inspect your tent for any signs of wear and tear. Check the seams, zippers, and air valves to ensure everything works well. If you notice any issues, address them promptly to prevent further damage. Your Coody inflatable tent can provide years of reliable service with proper care.
